Green Eggs and Ham

'Do you like green eggs and ham?'

From Dr Seuss' 'Green Eggs and Ham', discover if you too would like here or there (or perhaps not anywhere.)

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 120g ham
  • 50ml green food colouring
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cook ham as per packet instructions.
  2. Separate egg yolks from whites. Add a drop of green food colouring to the yolk and gently spread until yolk is green. Dab off excess.
  3. Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a pan, pour in the egg whites. Once the whites begin to firm, add the yolk on top. Cook to your liking.
  4. Dab green food colouring onto cooked ham, add salt to taste and serve.
  5. Discover if you like green eggs and ham.
